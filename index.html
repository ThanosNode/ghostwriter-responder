FAQ

Common questions

How does the free trial work? +
Your free trial starts automatically the moment you complete checkout — 14 days on Monthly, 30 days on Annual. You won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel before then and you pay nothing.
Does it work on both Google Business and Yelp? +
Yes — GhostWriter injects a "Draft Smart Reply" button into both Google Business and Yelp review management pages automatically. One extension covers both platforms.
Will the replies sound like a robot? +
No. GhostWriter uses Anthropic's Claude AI which produces genuinely human-sounding, empathetic responses. You also choose your brand tone so every reply feels consistent with your voice.
Does it post replies automatically? +
Never. GhostWriter fills the reply box for you to review — you always have final approval before anything gets posted.
What is the difference between Monthly and Annual? +
Both plans include identical features. Annual is billed once at $60/year — that's $5/month, saving you $12 versus monthly. Annual also includes a longer 30-day free trial vs 14 days on Monthly.
How do I cancel? +
Log into your Stripe Customer Portal from the footer link. Cancel in two clicks — your subscription ends at the current billing period with no questions asked.
